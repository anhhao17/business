## Recommended Starting Strategy

### Phase 1: Start Simple
1. **Smart plugs** - Low complexity, high volume, EU plug compatible
2. **Smart switches** - Good technical differentiation, 200K-500K VND sweet spot
3. **Sensors** - Easy to customize firmware, growing security awareness

### Phase 2: Expand
4. **LED controllers/strips** - Popular for decoration, cafes
5. **Smart cameras** - Security demand, higher ticket price

### Phase 3: Premium
6. **Smart locks** - High margin, rental market
7. **Smart curtain motors** - Luxury segment, condo market
