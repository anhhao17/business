## Key Findings

### Deposit Requirements
- Most agents require **70% deposit** upfront (some 60-80%)
- Minimum service fees: 5,000-10,000 VND per order

### Exchange Rates
- All agents use their own CNY/VND rates (typically 3,990-4,050 VND per CNY)
- Compare rates when choosing agent - small differences add up

### 2025-2026 Tax Changes ⚠️
- **Feb 18, 2025**: VAT exemption removed for imports under 1M VND via express
- **May 21, 2025**: Taobao/1688 stopped direct shipping to Vietnam
- **Formal import (chính ngạch) is now recommended** even for small orders

## Recommendation for Hao ($500-2000 budget)

### Phase 1: Start (first 3 orders)
**Use Mã Tốc Logistics or AliOrder**
- 1% fee, supports all platforms
- No minimum order
- Chính ngạch available for compliance
- Contact 2-3 agents, compare quotes for your first order

### Phase 2: Scale (orders >$2000)
**Switch to ODN or Hoa Nam Logistics**
- Official 1688/Taobao partner
- Full VAT invoices, CO Form E
- Better for building legal paper trail

### Phase 3: Bulk (orders >$5000)
**Use Phương Khang Logistics**
- Cheapest bulk shipping (3,000 VND/kg)
- Chính ngạch specialist
- Best margins at scale

## How to Get a Quote from an Agent

1. Find product link on 1688/Taobao/Alibaba
2. Copy the full URL
3. Send to agent via:
   - Their website order form
   - Facebook/Zalo message
   - Email
4. Include: product link, quantity, color/size preferences
5. Agent replies with quote: product cost + service fee + shipping + estimated delivery
6. Pay deposit (usually 70%)
7. Agent buys, consolidates at China warehouse, ships to VN
8. Pay remaining balance + receive goods
