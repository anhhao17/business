## 4. Sourcing Strategy

### 4.1 Platform Comparison

| Platform | Best For | Price Level | MOQ | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1688.com** | Bulk wholesale | **Lowest** (20-40% cheaper) | 1+ carton | Hard (Chinese only, needs agent) |
| **Taobao** | Samples & small orders | Medium | 1 piece | Medium (needs agent) |
| **Alibaba.com** | International B2B | Slightly higher | 50-500 pcs | Easy (English, direct ship) |
| Pinduoduo | Ultra-cheap retail | Lowest | 1 piece | Hard (Chinese only) |

### 4.2 Recommended Sourcing Flow

```
Phase 1 ($500-2000):
  Taobao (via agent) → Order samples → Test quality → Small batch (10-50 pcs)

Phase 2 ($2000-5000):
  1688 (via agent) → Bulk orders (100+ pcs) → Better margins

Phase 3 ($5000+):
  1688 direct factory negotiation → Custom packaging → Build brand
```

### 4.3 Vietnam-Based Ordering Agents

Use an agent to handle: Chinese payment, domestic China shipping, customs, Vietnam delivery.

| Agent | Services | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| MICAFI | Taobao, 1688, Tmall | Quality check at China warehouse, ~7 day delivery |
| Viet Trung Company | Formal import, full docs | Good for scaling, VAT invoices |
| Ship Trung Việt | Multi-platform ordering | Popular with VN sellers |

**Agent fee structure** (typical):
- Product cost: at source price
- Service fee: 5-10% of product value
- International shipping: by weight (kg), ~$2-5/kg
- Customs: included or separate (ask upfront)

### 4.4 Sourcing Checklist

Before placing any order:

- [ ] Supplier has "Verified Supplier" badge
- [ ] Check supplier reviews & transaction history
- [ ] Request samples first (1-2 pieces)
- [ ] Negotiate price & MOQ
- [ ] Confirm shipping method & timeline
- [ ] Get product photos from supplier
- [ ] Verify product compliance with VN regulations

## 5. Pricing & Profit Model

### 5.1 Target Unit Economics

| Cost Component | Amount (VND) | % of Sell Price |
|---|---|---|
| Product cost (sourcing) | 100,000 | 33% |
| Shipping (China → VN) | 15,000 | 5% |
| Marketing / Ads | 100,000 | 33% |
| Platform fee (Shopee/TikTok) | 30,000 | 10% |
| Packaging & fulfillment | 15,000 | 5% |
| **Total cost** | **260,000** | **86%** |
| **Net profit** | **40,000** | **~14%** |

> **Rule**: Source price must be ≤ 33% of sell price. If you buy at 100K VND, sell at minimum 300K VND.

### 5.2 Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price Range (VND) | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| Impulse buy | 50K-100K | Volume play, easy TikTok conversion |
| **Sweet spot** | **100K-200K** | **Highest revenue tier, best balance** |
| Premium | 200K-500K | Need trust, reviews, brand building |
| High-end | 500K+ | Not for Phase 1 |

### 5.3 Sample P&L — Wireless Earbuds

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| Sourcing cost (1688) | $4 = ~100,000 VND |
| Shipping per unit | ~10,000 VND |
| Total landed cost | ~110,000 VND |
| Sell price (Shopee) | 299,000 VND |
| Gross margin | 189,000 VND (63%) |
| - Shopee fee (10%) | -29,900 VND |
| - Ads (33%) | -98,700 VND |
| - Packaging | -5,000 VND |
| **Net profit per unit** | **~55,400 VND (~18%)** |

> Scale to 500 units/month = ~$1,100/month net profit from one product.

## 6. Sales Channel Strategy

### 6.1 Shopee (Stable Volume)

**Setup**:
- Free seller account (need VN ID + bank account)
- Optimize listing: title keywords, clear photos, competitive price
- Join campaigns: 4.4, 5.5, 6.6, 11.11, 12.12 sales
- Aim for Shopee Mall once volume is consistent

**Tactics**:
- Price slightly below category average to get initial reviews
- Offer free shipping (absorb into price)
- Bundle deals (buy 2 get discount)
- Collect reviews aggressively (follow-up messages, small gifts)

### 6.2 TikTok Shop (Viral Growth)

**Setup**:
- Free seller account
- Link to Shopee for cross-promotion
- Focus on short demo videos + livestreams

**Content Strategy**:
- **Hook styles that work in VN**: "săn deal", "rẻ mà chất", "đáng tiền", "chốt đơn"
- **Video format**: Problem → Demo → Price → Call to action (under 30 sec)
- **Livestream**: 2-3x/week, show product in real-time, answer questions
- **Affiliate**: Recruit KOCs (key opinion consumers) - pay 10-20% commission
- **Treat TikTok as a search engine**: Optimize video titles/descriptions with keywords

**Content Ideas for Tech Products** (your edge):
- "Test pin tai nghe 80 giờ thực tế" (real battery test)
- "So sánh tai nghe rẻ vs đắt" (cheap vs expensive comparison)
- "Setup smart home với 500K" (smart home setup under $20)
- "Unbox + test chất lượng hàng xưởng" (factory quality unbox)

### 6.3 Cross-Channel Funnel

```
TikTok video (discovery)
    → TikTok Shop purchase (impulse)
    OR
    → Shopee search (trust building)
        → Shopee purchase (planned)

Retarget: Shopee buyers → TikTok follow → Repeat purchases
```

## 8. Operations

### 8.1 Order Fulfillment

| Step | Detail |
|---|---|
| Packaging | Bubble wrap for electronics, branded sticker (cheap) |
| Shipping | Shopee SPX / TikTok Shop logistics (free for seller) |
| Lead time | Ship within 48 hours of order |
| Returns | Accept returns, factor 5-10% return rate into pricing |

### 8.2 Inventory Management

| Phase | Stock Level | Reorder Point |
|---|---|---|
| Test phase | 5-20 units | N/A (testing demand) |
| Early sales | 30-50 units | Reorder at 10 units |
| Stable sales | 100+ units | Reorder at 30 units |

> **Rule**: Never tie up more than 50% of capital in inventory. Keep cash for ads and new product tests.

### 8.3 Quality Control (your edge)

- Test every new product batch yourself before listing
- Check: build quality, battery life, charging speed, signal, durability
- Document test results → use as marketing content
- Reject bad batches from supplier → negotiate replacement

## 10. Risk Management

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Bad quality products | Test samples first, never bulk order untested |
| Shipping delays | Order 2 weeks before sales campaigns |
| Customs issues | Use reputable agent, declare properly |
| Ad spend waste | Cap daily budget, kill losers fast, scale winners |
| Platform policy changes | Diversify across Shopee + TikTok |
| Copycat competitors | Build brand via content, reviews, bundles |
| Cash flow crunch | Keep 30% cash reserve, don't over-invest in stock |
| Regulatory (cosmetics) | Start with tech accessories (lower regulation), research before beauty |

## 14. Lessons & Reminders

- **Start small, test fast, scale what works**
- **Your tech background is your moat — use it for content and quality control**
- **TikTok is a search engine now** — optimize video titles with Vietnamese keywords
- **Price framing matters**: "rẻ mà chất" beats "cheap" in Vietnamese buyer psychology

- **Never run out of stock on a winner** — but never overstock a loser
- **Keep 30% cash reserve** always
- **Ads are expensive** — only scale products with proven organic demand
