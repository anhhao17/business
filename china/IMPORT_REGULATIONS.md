# Vietnam Import Regulations Guide

> Researched: 2026-07-13 | For: Hộ kinh doanh, $500-2000 budget

---

## ⚠️ Critical Timeline Changes

| Date | Change | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Feb 18, 2025** | VAT exemption removed for imports under 1M VND via express | All small parcels now pay VAT |
| **May 21, 2025** | Taobao/1688 stopped direct shipping to VN | Must use intermediary warehouses in China |
| **Jan 1, 2029** | Border residents must be present for customs | Cannot proxy for commercial buyers |
| **Jan 1, 2030** | 100% formal channels required | Informal import (tiểu ngạch) effectively banned |

**For your budget**: Formal import (chính ngạch) is now the only viable long-term option.

## 1. Import Methods

### Chính Ngạch (Formal Import) ✅ Recommended
- Full legal documentation
- Customs declaration, VAT invoice, C/O Form E
- Required documents:
  - Foreign trade contract
  - Commercial invoice
  - Packing list
  - Bill of lading
  - C/O Form E (for China - gives 0% import tax)
  - Customs declaration

### Tiểu Ngạch (Informal/Border Trade) ⚠️ Ending
- Legal border trade within 20km of border
- Simplified procedures
- **Being phased out by 2030**
- Taobao/1688 direct shipping already stopped (May 2025)

### Recommendation for $500-2000 Budget
**Use customs brokerage service (dịch vụ ủy thác)**
- Handles formal import for small shipments
- Service fee: 500,000-2,000,000 VND per shipment
- Provides VAT invoices for platform compliance

## 2. Tax & Duties

### Import Tax Rates (with C/O Form E = 0% from China)

| Product | HS Code | MFN Tax | With C/O Form E | VAT |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Phone cases (plastic) | 3926.90.99 | 12% | **0%** | 8% |
| Phone cases (leather) | 4202.91.90 | 25% | **0%** | 8% |
| Charging cables | 8544.42.19 | 3-7% | **0%** | 8% |
| Power adapters | 8504.40.xx | 5-10% | **0%** | 8% |
| Smart hub/gateway | 8517.62.xx | 0% | **0%** | 8% |
| Smart speaker | 8518.22.90 | 20% | **0%** | 8% |
| Smart lock | 8301.40.20 | 25% | **0%** | 8% |
| Lipstick | 3304.10.00 | 20% | **0%** | 8% |
| Skincare creams | 3304.99.30 | 20% | **0%** | 8% |
| Acne treatment | 3304.99.20 | 10% | **0%** | 8% |
| Power bank | 8507.60.39 | 10-20% | **0%** | 10% |
| Lithium batteries | 8507.60.90 | 10-20% | **0%** | 10% |

> **CRITICAL**: Always obtain C/O Form E from Chinese suppliers → 0% import tax under ACFTA

### VAT Rates (2026)

| Rate | Applies To |
|---|---|
| 0% | Exports, international transport |
| 5% | Essential goods (water, fertilizer, medical) |
| **8%** | **Most goods** (reduced from 10%, valid until Dec 31, 2026) |
| 10% | Telecom, finance, banking, real estate, metals |

### Tax Calculation Formula

```
Import Tax = CIF Value × Tax Rate
VAT = (CIF Value + Import Tax) × VAT Rate
Total Tax = Import Tax + VAT

CIF = Cost (goods) + Insurance + Freight (shipping)
```

### Example: $1000 CIF (Phone accessories, with C/O Form E)
- Import Tax: $1000 × 0% = **$0**
- VAT: ($1000 + $0) × 8% = **$80**
- **Total Tax: $80**

### Example: $1000 CIF (WITHOUT C/O Form E)
- Import Tax: $1000 × 12% = **$120**
- VAT: ($1000 + $120) × 8% = **$89.60**
- **Total Tax: $209.60**

> Without C/O Form E, you pay 2.6x more tax. Always request it!

## 3. Product-Specific Regulations

### Electronics - QCVN 4:2009/BKHCN

**Mandatory conformity certification** for most electrical/electronic devices.

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Requirement | Chứng nhận hợp quy (conformity certification) |
| Testing | QUATEST, VINACOM, VINACONTROL |
| Mark | CR mark must be affixed |
| Labeling | Vietnamese language required |
| Cost | 2-5 million VND per product model |
| Timeline | 15-30 working days |

**Process**:
1. Submit product samples (min 3 units)
2. Testing at designated lab
3. Certificate of conformity issued
4. Declaration of conformity (công bố hợp quy)
5. Affix CR mark

### Batteries/Power Banks - QCVN 101:2020/BTTTT ⚠️ High Risk

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Requirement | Mandatory testing + conformity declaration |
| Documents | UN38.3 testing summary, MSDS, battery specs |
| Cost | 3-7 million VND per model |
| Timeline | 20-40 working days |
| **USED power banks** | **BANNED from import** |
| Shipping | Dangerous goods (IATA), air shipping restricted |

> **Recommendation**: AVOID power banks initially. High compliance cost + shipping restrictions.

### Cosmetics - Thông báo mỹ phẩm ⚠️ High Cost

**Mandatory** under Thông tư 06/2011/TT-BYT

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Required before | Goods arrive at customs |
| Valid for | 5 years |
| Government fee | 500,000 VND per product |
| Service fee (agency) | 2,000,000-3,300,000 VND per product |
| Timeline | 3 working days (approval), 30-45 days total |

**Required documents**:
1. Product notification form
2. Power of attorney from manufacturer
3. Certificate of free sale
4. Product specification
5. Label sample

> **Recommendation**: AVOID cosmetics initially. High certification cost per product.

### LED Lights - QCVN 19:2019/BKHCN

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Requirement | Quality inspection + energy efficiency testing |
| Standards | TCVN 7722-1 (IEC 60598-1), TCVN 13079-5:2020 |
| Cost | 3-6 million VND per model |
| Timeline | 20-30 working days |
| **USED LED lights** | **BANNED from import** |
| Energy labeling | Mandatory |

### Bluetooth/WiFi Devices - QCVN 54:2020/BTTTT ⚠️ Required for Smart Home

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Requirement | RF certification by Ministry of Information (MIC) |
| Applies to | ALL Bluetooth/WiFi devices (earbuds, smart plugs, cameras) |
| Testing | RF + EMC testing |
| Mark | ICT Label must be affixed |
| Cost | 5-10 million VND per device model |
| Timeline | 25-45 working days |

> **Important**: Even smart plugs with WiFi require this certification.

### Smart Plugs/Electrical - Combined Requirements

| Certification | Cost | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Electrical safety (QCVN 4) | $80-200 | 15-30 days |
| Wireless (QCVN 54) | $200-400 | 25-45 days |
| **Combined** | **$280-600 (8-15M VND)** | **30-50 days** |

## 4. Business Registration

### Can You Import Without a Company?

**Technically yes** (for personal use), but **NO for commercial selling**.

For commercial purposes, you need:
- **Hộ kinh doanh** (Household Business) - simplest, recommended
- OR **Doanh nghiệp** (Company) - for scaling

### Hộ Kinh Doanh Registration

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Cost | **50,000 VND** (in-person) or **FREE** (online) |
| Service fee (if using agency) | 300,000-800,000 VND |
| Timeline | 3 working days |
| Where | Local commune/ward (UBND cấp xã) or dichvucong.gov.vn |

**Steps**:
1. Prepare: Application form + ID card copy + lease agreement
2. Submit to local authority or online portal
3. Pay fee (50K or free online)
4. Receive certificate (Giấy chứng nhận đăng ký hộ kinh doanh)
5. Tax registration (automatic)

**Total cost**: 100,000-950,000 VND

### When to Upgrade to Company

| Choose Hộ Kinh Doanh if... | Choose Company if... |
|---|---|
| Revenue < 100 billion VND/year | Revenue > 100 billion VND/year |
| Single location | Multiple locations |
| Simple structure | Need limited liability |
| Low admin cost | Seeking investment |

> For Shopee/TikTok selling: **Hộ kinh doanh is sufficient** initially.

## 5. Tax Obligations for E-commerce Sellers

### New Platform Withholding System (from July 1, 2025)

**Nghị định 117/2025/NĐ-CP**: Platforms MUST withhold tax automatically.

| Tax | Rate | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| VAT | 1% | Gross revenue |
| Personal Income Tax | 0.5% | Gross revenue |
| **Total withheld** | **1.5%** | **Gross revenue** |

**Example**:
- Sale: 1,000,000 VND
- Platform fee: 50,000 VND
- Tax withheld: 1,000,000 × 1.5% = 15,000 VND
- **You receive**: 1,000,000 - 50,000 - 15,000 = **935,000 VND**

> Tax is calculated on GROSS revenue (before platform fees).

### Tax Thresholds (2026)

| Annual Revenue | Tax Treatment |
|---|---|
| Under 200M VND | **Exempt** from VAT and PIT |
| 200M-500M VND | 1.5% withheld by platform |
| 500M-3B VND | 1.5% + may need annual finalization |
| Over 3B VND | Must switch to profit-based taxation (17%) |

### License Fee (Lệ phí môn bài)

| Revenue | Annual Fee |
|---|---|
| Under 100M VND | Exempt |
| 100M-300M VND | 300,000 VND |
| 300M-500M VND | 500,000 VND |
| 500M-1B VND | 1,000,000 VND |

## 6. Platform Requirements

### Shopee Seller Requirements

**Individual**:
- ID card/CCCD
- Tax code (mã số thuế)
- Bank account (same name as ID)
- Phone + address

**Household Business** (recommended):
- Business registration certificate
- Tax code
- Bank account in business name
- Owner's ID

**Shopee Mall** (later):
- Business registration mandatory
- Brand registration or distribution authorization
- Product origin documentation
- Quality certificates for regulated products

### TikTok Shop Seller Requirements

**Individual**:
- Vietnamese citizen, 18+
- Valid ID card/CCCD
- Bank account (same name)
- TikTok account

**Household Business**:
- Business registration certificate
- Tax code
- Bank account in business name

**Key restrictions**:
- **One shop per CCCD** (each ID = one TikTok Shop only)
- Vietnamese citizens only
- Strict AI review of documents
- Approval: 24-48 hours

### Product Listing Requirements (Nghị định 85/2021/NĐ-CP)

All listings must include:
1. Product name
2. Name + address of responsible organization/individual
3. Product origin (xuất xứ)
4. Specifications per product type

## 7. Step-by-Step Process for Your Situation

### Phase 1: Business Setup (Week 1) - Cost: ~200,000 VND

- [ ] Register Hộ kinh doanh (50K VND or free online)
- [ ] Obtain tax code (automatic with registration)
- [ ] Open business bank account (free)
- [ ] **Total: ~200,000 VND ($8)**

### Phase 2: Product Selection (Week 2)

**Products to START with** (low regulation):
- ✅ Phone cases (minimal regulation)
- ✅ Charging cables (simple)
- ✅ Screen protectors (no certification)
- ✅ Simple non-electronic accessories

**Products to AVOID initially** (high regulation):
- ❌ Cosmetics (2-3M VND per product certification)
- ❌ Power banks (battery regulations, shipping restrictions)
- ❌ Smart plugs with WiFi (need 8-15M VND certifications)
- ❌ Bluetooth devices (5-10M VND RF certification)
- ❌ LED lights (3-6M VND + energy labeling)

> **Note**: Smart home products require significant certification investment. Start with non-electronic accessories, then expand to smart home once revenue justifies the compliance cost.

### Phase 3: Import Process (Week 3-4)

**Use customs brokerage service** (recommended for beginners):
1. Find reputable broker: "dịch vụ ủy thác nhập khẩu hàng Trung Quốc"
2. Service includes: customs declaration, tax payment, documentation, delivery
3. Cost: 500,000-2,000,000 VND per shipment
4. **ALWAYS request C/O Form E** from supplier → 0% import tax

### Phase 4: Platform Setup (Week 4)

1. Register Shopee seller account (1-3 days approval)
2. Register TikTok Shop seller account (24-48 hours)
3. Prepare product listings with required info
4. Set competitive pricing

### Phase 5: Ongoing Compliance

- **Monthly**: Monitor revenue, platform withholds 1.5% tax automatically
- **Quarterly/Annually**: Tax finalization if revenue > 200M VND
- **License fee**: Annual payment based on revenue tier

## ⚠️ Important Reality Check

The smart home products in our CSV look attractive, but **most require expensive certifications**:
- Smart plugs with WiFi: 8-15M VND per model
- Bluetooth earbuds: 5-10M VND per model
- LED lights: 3-6M VND per model

**For your $500-2000 budget**, start with:
1. **Phone cases** - no certification needed, good margins
2. **Charging cables** - simple, high demand
3. **Non-electronic accessories** - screen protectors, stands, holders

Then, once you have steady revenue (3-6 months), invest in certifications for smart home products where your technical background gives you a real edge.

> Regulations change frequently. Always verify with official sources before making business decisions.
