## 3. TikTok Video Scripts (3 per product)

### Script Template: Problem → Solution → Demo → CTA

```
[0-3s] HOOK: Show the problem (hot weather, dead phone, etc.)
[3-8s] INTRO: "Mình tìm thấy cái này..." 
[8-15s] DEMO: Show product working, close-up
[15-20s] FEATURES: 3 key benefits (text overlay)
[20-25s] PRICE: "Chỉ [XX]K, freeship nhé"
[25-30s] CTA: "Bấm vào giỏ hàng ngay / Link ở bio"
```

## 4. Livestream Script Template

### Pre-Livestream Checklist
- [ ] Products ready on table (3-5 products)
- [ ] Phone charged + backup battery
- [ ] Ring light / good lighting
- [ ] Stable phone stand
- [ ] Water nearby
- [ ] Notepad for orders
- [ ] Background clean and tidy

### Livestream Run Sheet (60 minutes)

| Time | Segment | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 0-5 min | Welcome | Greet viewers, introduce shop, today's deals |
| 5-15 min | Product 1 demo | Show, test, explain features, answer questions |
| 15-20 min | Q&A + Flash deal | Limited time coupon for first 10 buyers |
| 20-30 min | Product 2 demo | Show, test, compare with Product 1 |
| 30-35 min | Q&A + Bundle deal | Buy 2 get discount |
| 35-45 min | Product 3 demo | Show, test, real-time usage |
| 45-50 min | Q&A + Last call | Final coupon, urgency |
| 50-60 min | Wrap up | Thank viewers, announce next stream, follow CTA |

### Livestream Hook Phrases (Vietnamese)

```
"Mấy bạn ơi, deal này chỉ có trên live thôi!"
"Còn [X] suất, chốt nhanh kẻo hết"
"Test thật nha, không nói quá đâu"
"Mua 2 cái giảm thêm [X]K nè"
"Follow shop để nhận voucher nhé"
"Ai hỏi gì comment bên dưới nha"
"Freeship cho 10 bạn đầu tiên bấm giỏ hàng"
```

## 6. Pricing Strategy by Platform

| Platform | Pricing Approach |
|---|---|
| **Shopee** | Price slightly below category average. Use "original price" (crossed out) higher to show discount. Join platform sales (4.4, 5.5, 11.11). |
| **TikTok Shop** | Can price slightly higher than Shopee (impulse buy). Use livestream-only flash deals. Bundle with free shipping. |

### Price Anchoring Example (Neck Fan)

| Display | Price | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| "Original price" (crossed out) | 249,000 | Anchor - makes deal look better |
| Selling price | 149,000 | Actual price |
| "Flash sale" (livestream only) | 129,000 | Urgency during livestream |
| Competitor average | 160,000 | Stay below this |

## 7. First 30 Days Content Calendar

| Week | Shopee | TikTok | Livestream |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | List 3 products, optimize titles/images | Post 5 videos (1 per product + 1 intro) | 2 livestreams (30 min each) |
| Week 2 | Start Shopee ads ($5/day), collect first reviews | Post 5 videos (demos + tests) | 2 livestreams (45 min) |
| Week 3 | Optimize ads, adjust prices | Post 7 videos (trending sounds + products) | 3 livestreams (60 min) |
| Week 4 | Analyze data, kill losers, restock winners | Post 7 videos + 2 KOC collab videos | 3 livestreams (60 min) |

### Content Pillars (rotate these)

| Pillar | % of Content | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Product demo/test | 40% | "Test pin tai nghe 24h thực tế" |
| Educational/how-to | 25% | "Cách setup ổ cắm thông minh 2 phút" |
